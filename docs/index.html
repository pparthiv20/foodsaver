About Food Saver

Food Saver is a community-driven platform that helps restaurants and donors share excess food with NGOs and individuals in need.

Key Features

  • ✅ Restaurant donations management
  • ✅ NGO profile and food request system
  • ✅ User dashboard and donation tracking
  • ✅ Admin analytics and reporting
  • ✅ Email notifications
